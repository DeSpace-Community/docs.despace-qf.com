DeSpace QF initiative supports decentralized projects related to space, to push
for the betterment of technology and commercialization. It applies solidarity
principles and is greatly inspired by regenerative finance principles.

The community is composed of project leaders, project supporters and
contributors, investors, space fans and other external supports.

You, me, them, everybody.
